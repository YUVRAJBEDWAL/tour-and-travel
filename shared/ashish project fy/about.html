About CityCalendar

Your Ultimate Event Discovery Platform

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CityCalendar emerged from a simple idea: making it easier for people to discover and participate in local events. What started as a small project has grown into a vibrant platform connecting event organizers with enthusiastic attendees.

Our mission is to enrich communities by facilitating meaningful connections through shared experiences and events.

Our Values

Community First

Trust & Safety

Your security and satisfaction are our top priorities in every event.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform to enhance your event experience.

Collaboration

We work closely with organizers to create successful events.
